Welcome to my tiny slice of the internet. It is small and a little messy, but it is mine and I like it that way. Think of this place as a mix of a workshop, a notebook, and whatever else I feel like adding.

This whole thing started as a class assignment. Then I kept adding to it until it turned into a personal site where I can show off projects, talk about the things I enjoy, and maybe write a blog post if I am not busy staring at a terminal.

Feel free to look around. There is a good chance you will find something odd, something nerdy, or something I broke on purpose.
